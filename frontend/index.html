Community Guide

Welcome! Our platform is built on mutual respect. Please keep conversations friendly and constructive at all times.

Profile Authenticity: Use real photos and accurate personal information. Fake profiles or impersonation accounts are permanently banned.

Respect First: Harassment, hate speech, threats, or abusive language toward other users is strictly prohibited.

Content Standards: Explicit or nude content is not encouraged. Content deemed excessive, exploitative, or unsafe may result in warnings or bans.

No Scamming: Financial requests, fraud attempts, or manipulation for money or services will lead to immediate account termination.

WhatsApp Responsibility: Once redirected to WhatsApp, interactions remain your responsibility. Report misconduct immediately.

Age Compliance: Users must meet the legal age requirement. Underage accounts are removed without notice.

Spam Policy: Mass messaging, advertising, or promotional links are not allowed on the platform.

Account Security: Sharing accounts or allowing third-party access compromises safety and may result in suspension.

Respect Intentions: Misleading users about relationship intentions or identity may lead to account review.

Reporting Matters: Users are encouraged to report suspicious or harmful behavior to help keep the community safe.

Warning System: Policy violations may result in a 24-hour account suspension as an official warning.

Ban Enforcement: Serious or repeated violations lead to permanent bans and immediate account logout.

Multiple Accounts: Creating new accounts to bypass restrictions or bans is prohibited.

Privacy Protection: Do not share another user’s personal information without consent.

Community Safety: The platform reserves the right to review, restrict, or remove accounts to maintain a secure environment.

Safety & Tips

Verification: Always look for the verified badge. It means the user has passed our basic identity check.

Save Your Login Details: Please save your email and password safely. Account recovery is currently unavailable, and lost credentials may result in permanent loss of your account.

Complete Your Profile: After creating your account, complete your profile by adding your WhatsApp number, age, intentions, profile picture, and up to three gallery photos.

Use a Real WhatsApp Number: Add an active WhatsApp number that belongs to you. This is the number other users will use to contact you.

Add Clear Photos: A profile picture is required to activate your account. You may upload a maximum of three gallery photos.

Photo Updates: Your profile picture can be changed every 3 days. Gallery photos can only be added once, so choose carefully.

Save Changes: Always press the save button after updating your profile to ensure your information is visible to others.

Account Types: Completed profiles start as Normal accounts with limited access. Subscription tiers include Premium, Legend, and Elite, each unlocking additional features.

Viewing Profiles: You can explore other users by opening their profiles to learn more about them before connecting.

Connecting on WhatsApp: Clicking the WhatsApp button redirects you directly to the user’s WhatsApp chat if your account tier allows access.

Access Restrictions: Normal accounts may encounter access limits. Upgrading your subscription removes these restrictions.

Payments: Ensure the WhatsApp number saved on your profile supports M-Pesa before making a subscription payment.

Payment Confirmation: When subscribing, you will receive a payment request on your phone. Complete the prompt to activate your selected tier.

Report Suspicious Users: Immediately report accounts involved in scams, impersonation, extortion, or misleading behavior.

Stay Safe: Avoid sharing sensitive personal or financial information with users you have just met.

Contact Admin Support: For complaints, issues, or account concerns, use the Admin support option available within the app.

Community Responsibility: Respect other users and help maintain a safe, genuine, and enjoyable dating environment for everyone.
